Children’s Health

Trusted resources for topics relating to children's health.

  • American Academy of Pediatrics (AAP)
    A trusted authority offering evidence-based guidance on pediatric care, child development, and preventative health.
  • CDC Child Development and Health
    Provides essential information on children’s growth stages, vaccinations, safety, and health promotion.
  • AAP: Healthy Children
    A family focused site offering practical health tips, parenting advice and wellness resources for children of all ages.
  • Children’s Health Insurance Program (CHIP)
    A federal program that provides low-cost health coverage to children in families that earn too much to qualify for Medicaid.
  • Nemours KidsHealth
    Offers child-friendly articles, videos, and educational tools on physical and mental health tailored for kids, teens, and parents.
  • HRSA Maternal and Child Health Bureau
    Supports national programs that improve the health and well-being of mothers, infants, and children through services and research.
