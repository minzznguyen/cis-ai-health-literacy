Medical Law

IMAGE

Trusted resources for topics relating to medical laws.

  • American Health Law Association
    A leading educational organization focused on legal issues in the health care, providing resources for providing for professionals navigating health law
  • American Bar Association (ABA): Health Law Section
    A national network offering guidance, policy updates, and legal insights for attorneys working at the intersection of law and healthcare.
  • USF Medicine & Law Library
    A specialized collection of publications and journals supporting research in both medical and legal fields
  • PubMed
    A free resource from National Institutes of Health offering access to a vast database of biomedical literature, including legal and ethical topics in medicine.
  • Health Affairs: Health Policy & Law
    A leading peer-reviewed journal that explores the intersection of health policy, ethics, and law in U.S. and global healthcare systems
  • National Health Law Program (NHeLP)
    A nonprofit organization working to protect and advance health rights of low income populations through legal and policy advocacy.
