Community Health

Trusted resources for topics relating to community health.

  • Community Health (Wikipedia)
    This entry gives an overview of what community health means, its importance, the types of services involved (preventive, promotive, curative, rehabilitative), and how it fits into public health-an accessible starting point for anyone new to the topic.
  • Fundamentals – Community Health (YouTube Playlist)
    A series of short videos covering core concepts, terminology, and roles in community health, making it easy to get a visual and practical introduction to the field.
  • A Beginner's Guide to Building Your First Community Health Worker Program
    This guide outlines the essential skills and steps for starting a community health worker program, including communication, relationship-building, and service coordination-perfect for learning about the practical side of community health.
  • Introduction to Community Health Outreach (ArcGIS Solutions)
    This resource explains how community health outreach programs work, why they matter, and how they help connect people to health services and key health initiatives in their area.
