Research

Trusted resources for topics relating to medical research.

  • PubMed
    A free resource developed by the National Library of Medicine that provides access to over 35 million citations for biomedical literature, including research articles, reviews, and clinical studies.
  • National Institutes of Health (NIH)
    The U.S. government’s primary medical research agency, funding and publishing cutting-edge research on health, diseases, treatments, and public health policy.
  • Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC)
    The CDC offers up-to-date public health information, disease statistics, and health promotion research, trusted globally for its evidence-based guidance.
  • World Health Organization (WHO)
    A global health authority that publishes international health research, disease surveillance, policy guidance, and data on health systems and outcomes.
  • HealthData.gov
    An open government platform offering access to high-value health datasets from U.S. federal agencies for public use and research.
