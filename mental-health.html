Mental Health

Trusted resources for topics relating to mental health.

  • Mental Health Basics (Healthline)
    This article covers what mental health is, common mental illnesses, how mental health is diagnosed and treated, and tips for maintaining good mental health-an excellent primer for beginners.
  • Mental Health: Definition, Common Disorders, Early Signs, and More (Medical News Today)
    This resource explains the meaning of mental health, its impact on daily life, common disorders, and factors that affect mental well-being, making it easy to understand the essentials.
  • NIMH Mental Health Resources for Students and Educators
    The National Institute of Mental Health offers free videos, activity books, and quizzes designed to help students and educators learn about mental health in an engaging way.
  • World Health Organization: What is Mental Health?
    The WHO defines mental health, describes its importance for individuals and communities, and outlines factors that influence mental well-being, providing a trusted foundation for further learning.
